Your Game Day Circuit

Morning: Lava Lava Beach Club

Start your day with sand between your toes and the ocean breeze. Lava Lava opens early at 7am, perfect for catching those East Coast noon kickoffs with breakfast and bottomless mimosas. The beachfront setting sets a relaxed tone before the intensity builds.

Game day move: Grab a beach-side table early. Order the loco moco for substance, and their legendary Mai Tai for celebration. The morning crowd is mellow - perfect for easing into the day.

Afternoon: Bobby V's Bourbon House

When the afternoon games heat up, move inside to Bobby V's. They've got Kauai's largest bourbon selection and enough TVs to catch every game. The vibe shifts from beach casual to sports bar energy, with hearty pizzas and burgers to soak up those bourbons.

Pro move: Try their bourbon flight during halftime. The garlic knots are legendary here, and the garden patio offers a breather between games if you need some air.

Evening: Johnny's Wingz 'N Tingz

Cap off your Sunday at Johnny's - the island's undisputed wing champion. The late-night vibe here is electric, with locals getting rowdy for primetime games. Their wings are crispy outside, juicy inside, and the sauce selection is unmatched on the island.

Wing wisdom: Half honey garlic, half buffalo is the move. They stay open late, the crowd gets passionate about their teams, and the energy is contagious.
