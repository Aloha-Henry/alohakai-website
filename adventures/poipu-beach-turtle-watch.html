Your Beach to Dinner Circuit

Main: All Day at Poipu Beach Park

Spend your day at America's best beach (2001 winner) where green sea turtles lounge on shore and monk seals sometimes appear. Perfect combo of calm snorkeling areas and reef breaks, plus lifeguards for safety.

Award-winning magic: There's a real reason it won that title - something for everyone from families to adventure seekers, plus the wildlife encounters are legendary.

Fuel: Puka Dog

When hunger hits, grab a unique savory treat at nearby Puka Dog (open daily 10am-7pm). These aren't your average hot dogs - they're a local island twist that'll keep you fueled for more beach time.

Local twist: Perfect beach food - portable, satisfying, and totally unique to Hawaii. Right near the beach so you don't lose your perfect spot.

The Iconic Turtle Hour

Head back to the beach for that magical moment when green sea turtles come ashore during sunset. This is the moment everyone talks about.

The shower facilities at Poipu Beach let you rinse off the salt before dinner - perfect transition from beach day to evening dining.

End: Koloa Pizza Kitchen

Cap the perfect day with South Shore's best pizza. Exceptional pies with expansive menu, open until 9pm every night. Perfect whether you dine out and extend the social experience or grab takeout for cozy night back at your place.

Flexible finish: After a full beach day, you can choose your vibe - social dining out or relaxed takeout. Either way, you're getting the island's best pizza.
