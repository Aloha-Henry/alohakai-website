Your Perfect North Shore Day

Morning

Anini Beach Snorkeling

Anini offers one of Kauai's most family-friendly snorkeling spots - thanks to its protective reef, shallow calm waters, and clear visibility. The water remains peaceful year-round, but is especially clear and serene early in the morning before winds kick up.

🏊‍♀️ Protected Reef 🌅 Early Clarity 👶 Family Friendly
Lunch

Kilauea Fish Market

After snorkeling, head to Kilauea Fish Market (open Mon–Sat, 11 am–8 pm) for some of the best grilled Mahi-Mahi fish tacos on the island - generous portions atop flour tortillas with fresh toppings. Reviews hail them as "to die for," and the market is commonly listed among Kauai's top fish-taco spots.

🐟 Fresh Mahi-Mahi 🌮 Generous Portions ⭐ Top Rated
Afternoon

Kilauea Point Lighthouse

Finally, take a short drive to the iconic Kilauea Point Lighthouse. Since access is regulated, be sure to book your visit in advance via recreation.gov. It's a classic landmark with epic coastal views - a perfect cap for this exploration loop.

🏛️ Historic Landmark 📅 Book Ahead 🌊 Epic Views

Henry's Insider Tips

Local knowledge you won't find in guidebooks

Early Bird Advantage

Get to Anini early morning for the clearest water visibility before afternoon winds stir things up. The protected reef keeps conditions calm, but morning is magical.

Fish Market Timing

Kilauea Fish Market opens at 11am - perfect timing after your morning snorkel session. The fish tacos really are as good as everyone says.

Lighthouse Reservations

Don't forget to book your lighthouse visit on recreation.gov in advance. Access is regulated, so you can't just show up. But the coastal views are worth the planning.

Beach Amenities

Anini has restrooms, showers, shaded picnic tables, and picnic lawns - making pre- or post-snorkel lounging a breeze. Great for families.
