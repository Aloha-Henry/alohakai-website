Your Wellness Circuit

Start: Sunrise Yoga at Pine Trees, Hanalei Bay

Begin before dawn with an almost-empty beach fronting Hanalei Bay - a bow-shaped stretch surrounded by lush mountains. Park in the Pine Trees lot on Ama Ama Road, where you'll find a mural honoring surfing legend Andy Irons.

Sunrise magic: Perfect meditative flow as sun rises over tranquil waters - ideal setting for chaturanga and mindful movement before the world wakes up.

Next: Chill Time in Hanalei Town

After your practice, drift into town for grounded community time. Sip locally roasted coffee, browse hand-crafted art, or wander botanical gardens for peaceful reflection - perfect low-key transition.

Cultural flow: The transition from beach yoga to town wandering creates perfect rhythm - physical practice to cultural immersion to wellness finale.

Choose Your Afternoon Wellness

To extend your wellness journey, choose from two standout options based on your vibe

Black Coral Yoga Studio

Serene studio offering Heated Vinyasa, Hatha with Sound Healing, and Sculpt. The heated classes (88-92°F via infrared) deepen muscle release, while meditative offerings provide restorative flow.

Heated Classes Sound Healing Sculpt Classes

Bamford Wellness Spa at 1 Hotel

Luxury wellness sanctuary blending ancient Hawaiian techniques with holistic therapies. Peaceful follow-up to your beach-side awakening, set within rich, sustainable design.
