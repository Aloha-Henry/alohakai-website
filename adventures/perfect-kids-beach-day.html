Your Family Beach Circuit

Start: Sweet Treats at Passion Bakery

Fresh malasadas (Portuguese-style fried donuts) baked daily - the perfect sugar boost before beach time with keiki. These sweet treats get everyone excited for the day ahead.

Family win: Fresh daily, kid-approved sweetness, coffee for parents. Open Tuesday-Sunday, 7am-3pm or until sold out - so get there early!

Next: Easy Access to Lae Nani Beach

Park along Papaloa Road - short, stroller-friendly walk to the beach. Look for friendly local chickens along the path - kids love this bonus adventure before hitting the sand!

Stroller strategy: Shortest, smoothest path to sand. The walk is part of the adventure - kids love spotting the local chickens roaming around!

Main: Protected Lagoon Play

Natural lava rock reef creates a sheltered lagoon - calm, shallow waters perfect for toddlers. Parents can relax while kids splash safely in paradise. The reef protection makes this a natural kiddie pool.

Safety magic: Even at high tide, it stays shallow and calm. Perfect for nervous parents and fearless toddlers. Morning visits mean fewer people and better photos.

End: Beachfront Lunch at Lava Lava

The only beachfront dining during naptime hours! Stretch out on their family-friendly grassy lawn while munching, or let kids burn energy under easy supervision. Game-changer for families.

Parent hack: The grassy lawn lets kids run around while parents actually finish their food. Open during those tricky lunch-naptime hours when everywhere else is closed.
