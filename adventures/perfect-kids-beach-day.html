Henry's Family Beach Wisdom

Local parent knowledge you won't find in guidebooks

Stroller Strategy

Park on Papaloa Road for the shortest, smoothest path to sand. The walk is actually part of the adventure - kids love spotting the local chickens!

Protected Lagoon Magic

The lava rock reef creates a natural kiddie pool. Even at high tide, it stays shallow and calm - perfect for nervous parents and fearless toddlers.

Naptime-Friendly Dining

Lava Lava's grassy lawn is a game-changer. Kids can run around while parents actually finish their food. Plus they're open during those tricky lunch-naptime hours.

Morning is Best

Calm waters, cooler temps, and fewer people. Start early and you'll have the lagoon almost to yourselves for those perfect family photos.
