Your Perfect Surf Circuit

Start: Fuel Up at Hanalei Bread Company

Start your day right with fresh pastries, coffee, and local atmosphere. Grab some energy for the waves ahead - you'll need it! Don't skip this - surfing uses way more energy than you think.

Henry's move: Load up on carbs here. The pastries are fresh-baked daily, and the coffee will wake you up for those early waves. Locals know to fuel up first.

Next: Gear Up at Backdoor Surf Shop

Walk across the street to Backdoor Surf - the locals' choice for boards. Ask the crew about today's conditions - they know every wave on North Shore and surf here daily.

Insider tip: These guys surf every day and know exactly where the best waves are breaking. Don't be shy - they love helping beginners find the perfect spot.

Main: Surf Hanalei Bay

The money spot! Beginner-friendly waves, stunning mountain backdrop, and that classic Hawaii surf vibe. Even if it's raining, the shots will be epic - moody mountains and glassy water create pure magic.

Wave wisdom: Keep the board all day for same price. After lunch, you might catch that perfect afternoon session when everyone else has gone home.

End: Post-Surf Feast at Kalypso

Celebrate your surf session with cold drinks and satisfying food. Share stories with other surfers and soak in that post-wave glow. This is where the surf stories get better with each telling.

Local vibe: Order whatever sounds good - you earned it! The cold beer tastes extra crispy after a morning in the water, and the food hits different post-surf.
