Hanalei Surf Adventure

The perfect intro to North Shore surfing - breakfast, board rental, beginner waves, and legendary post-surf lunch

By Henry

Kauai Real Estate Agent & Local Surfer

www.alohahenry.com
🏄‍♂️ Surf 📍 Hanalei ⏰ Half Day 👶 Beginner

🌊 Why This Adventure Rocks

Perfect for Beginners

Hanalei Bay offers the most forgiving waves on the North Shore. Protected by the reef, it's where locals learn to surf.

Local Business Circuit

Support three beloved Hanalei institutions in one perfect day - from breakfast to boards to beers.

Insider Timing

Early breakfast means first dibs on boards and waves before the crowds hit. Henry's lived here long enough to know the rhythm.

Epic Photo Ops

Dramatic mountain backdrop, pristine bay, and authentic surf culture. Your Instagram will never be the same.

Your Perfect Surf Day

7:00 AM

Fuel Up at Hanalei Bread Company

Start your day right with fresh pastries, coffee, and local atmosphere. Grab some energy for the waves ahead - you'll need it!

☕ Coffee 🥐 Pastries 🌅 Early Bird
8:00 AM

Gear Up at Backdoor Surf Shop

Walk across the street to Backdoor Surf - the locals' choice for boards. Ask the crew about today's conditions - they know every wave on the North Shore.

🏄‍♂️ Board Rental 👨‍🏫 Local Advice 🌊 Wave Check
9:00 AM

Surf Hanalei Pier

The money spot! Beginner-friendly waves, stunning mountain backdrop, and that classic Hawaii surf vibe. Even if it's raining, the shots will be epic!

🌊 Perfect Waves 📸 Epic Views 👶 Beginner Safe
12:00 PM

Post-Surf Feast at Kalypso

Celebrate your surf session with cold drinks and satisfying food. Share stories with other surfers and soak in that post-wave glow.

🍻 Cold Drinks 🍔 Hearty Food 🏄‍♂️ Surf Stories

Henry's Insider Tips

Local knowledge you won't find in guidebooks

Ask the Backdoor Crew

These guys surf every day and know exactly where the best waves are breaking today. Don't be shy - they love helping beginners find the perfect spot.

Rainy Day = Epic Photos

Don't let rain stop you! Some of the most dramatic surf shots happen when it's drizzling. The mountains get moody, the water gets glassy - pure magic.

Keep the Board All Day

Backdoor lets you keep boards all day for the same price. After lunch, you might catch that perfect afternoon session when everyone else has gone home.

Energy = Everything

Don't skip breakfast! Surfing uses way more energy than you think. Load up on carbs at Hanalei Bread - you'll thank me later.
